Frequently Asked Questions

Why separate tactical and strategic AI?

HR and managers need short-term actions (7-14 days). CEOs and boards need long-term decision prompts (60-120 days) with no tactical noise. We enforce this separation at the API level, not just the UI.

Can Team Intelligence users see leadership insights?

No. Leadership reports are completely separate. Even if a Team plan user tries to access leadership endpoints, they'll be blocked with a 403 error. This is a power boundary, not a feature toggle.

What happens when I upgrade from Team to Leadership?

You immediately unlock strategic AI, industry benchmarks, and leadership reports. We can optionally backfill up to 3 months of leadership reports. HR reports stay separate and unchanged.

Can I try Leadership Intelligence before upgrading?

Yes! All plans include a 30-day trial. You can start with Leadership Intelligence to see strategic insights, then downgrade if you only need tactical reporting.

Do leadership reports include individual names?

Never. Leadership reports use organizational-level synthesis only. Individual names, teams, and managers are aggregated into structural patterns. This is validated before every report is generated.

What's included in Organizational Intelligence (Custom)?

Board-level quarterly reviews, custom AI models, unlimited leadership roles, custom thresholds, and enterprise features like SSO and on-premise deployment. Contact us to discuss your needs.
