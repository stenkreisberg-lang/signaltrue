1. Behavioral Signals

We collect aggregated metadata from tools your team already uses. No content monitoring, no individual tracking.

What We Collect

  • Meeting frequency, duration, attendance counts
  • Message send times, thread response patterns
  • Calendar block types (meetings vs. focus time)
  • After-hours activity timestamps

What We DON'T Collect

  • Message content or sentiment analysis
  • Meeting recordings or transcripts
  • Individual performance scores
  • Email content or subject lines

Team #eng: 47 meetings this week (avg duration: 38 min)

2. Capability Indicators

Behavioral signals are transformed into 5 core capability indicators that measure structural health.

Resilience

Can the team restore capacity after disruption?

Calculation
After-hours recovery time + weekend disconnect rate

Execution Capacity

Can people do cognitively demanding work?

Calculation
Focus block duration - interruption frequency

Decision Speed

How quickly can the team make and communicate decisions?

Calculation
Thread response time + decision-to-action latency

Structural Health

Are dependencies and handoffs clean?

Calculation
Cross-team message ratio + handoff completion rate

Decision Closure Rate NEW

Does collaboration produce outcomes with clarity?

Calculation
(Meetings with action items + Messages with response) / Total collaboration events

3. Drift Detection

We compare current indicators to your team's baseline and flag sustained deviations.

What Is Drift?

A sustained change (5+ days) in a capability indicator that exceeds normal fluctuation.

We ignore short-term noise and focus on structural changes.
