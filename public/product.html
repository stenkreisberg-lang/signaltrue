Product

Make the system understandable, concrete, and safe.

SignalTrue helps leaders see when execution is starting to drift, early enough to act, without watching people.

Inputs – What data is used

SignalTrue works with tools you already use.

We analyze:

  • Calendar metadata (response timing, meeting load, focus fragmentation)
  • Collaboration metadata (volume, timing, rhythm changes)
  • All data is aggregated at team level.

We do not analyze:

  • Message content
  • Audio or video
  • Meeting transcripts
  • Individual behavior

Signals – What changes are detected

These do not mean failure. Together, they indicate execution friction.

SignalTrue detects patterns such as:

  • Slowing meeting acceptance
  • Growing meeting load
  • Increasing after-hours activity
  • Fragmented focus blocks

Signals matter only in context. So leaders can tell the difference between noise and drift.

Decisions – Not verdicts

SignalTrue never tells you what to do. You decide. SignalTrue supports.

It gives you:

  • A second opinion
  • Confidence to wait
  • Clarity to intervene
  • Micro-playbooks to reduce friction
