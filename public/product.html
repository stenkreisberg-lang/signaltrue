A Second Opinion, Not a Verdict

SignalTrue supports human judgment. It does not replace leadership intuition.

Data is a tool, not a master. Our signals are designed to be guidance—a "check engine light" for your organization—not automated decisions. You know your team best; we just help you see what you might be missing.

From data to decision — step by step

  1. Connect your tools: Calendar and collaboration metadata only.
  2. Establish a baseline: SignalTrue learns what “normal” looks like for each team.
  3. Detect drift: Changes in engagement, focus, and collaboration patterns.
  4. Explain why: Top contributing drivers are shown — no black box.
  5. Suggest actions: Contextual recommendations, not automation.
  6. Track impact: See whether actions actually improved outcomes.
