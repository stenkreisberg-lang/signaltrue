Product

A leadership decision support system for team health

SignalTrue helps leaders make better calls by turning collaboration patterns into explainable signals and clear next steps.

How it works

1. Connect your tools

Securely connect Slack and Calendar. No message content. No recordings. Metadata only.

2. Detect meaningful change

SignalTrue learns each team’s normal rhythm and flags sustained drift — not short-term noise.

3. Explain why it happened

Every alert includes the key drivers: meeting load, response pressure, after-hours work, and focus erosion.

4. Recommend what to do next

Micro playbooks suggest human actions leaders can take — not automated enforcement.

Core Capabilities

Drift Explainability

Understand why engagement or energy changed. No mystery charts. No guessing.

Micro Playbook Engine

Contextual recommendations like reducing meeting load, rebalancing communication pressure, or protecting focus time.

Program Impact Tracking

See whether leadership actions actually improved team health — not just whether they were executed.

Timeline Event Overlay

Understand how launches, reorganizations, or leadership changes affected team health over time.
