Why SignalTrue exists

Organizations fail quietly before they fail visibly. We built a system to detect the quiet failures.

Most organizational intelligence tools measure the past. They tell you what already happened: attrition rates, survey results, revenue dips. By the time these metrics move, the structural damage is done.

We measure the present. Specifically, we detect when organizational capabilities‚ÄîResilience, Execution Capacity, Decision Speed, Structural Health, and Decision Closure Rate‚Äîstart to degrade. These are leading indicators. They show what's breaking before outcomes slip.

But here's the problem: Most behavioral analytics tools destroy trust. They track individuals, read message content, create surveillance dashboards. Leaders become watchers. Teams become anxious.

That's not how we build.

SignalTrue is privacy-by-design. We measure team-level aggregated patterns, never individuals. We analyze metadata (meeting frequency, response times, calendar density), never content. We detect structural risk, not personal performance.

Organizations need leading indicators. But they need them built the right way.

Our Principles

Privacy-by-Design

Team-level aggregation only. No individual tracking, no content monitoring, no surveillance dashboards. We never show who sent a message or attended a meeting‚Äîonly that the team's patterns changed.

Leading, Not Lagging

We measure capability drift in real-time, not outcomes after the fact. By the time attrition or burnout shows up, you've lost people. We detect the structural issues before they cascade.

Context, Not Just Numbers

A metric without context is dangerous. Every drift alert includes the drivers (meeting load, focus erosion, after-hours creep), the context (recent reorg, product launch), and recommended actions.

Support Leaders, Don't Replace Them

We provide signals and playbook recommendations, but humans make the decisions. Automation works for factories. Organizations need judgment, empathy, and trust.
