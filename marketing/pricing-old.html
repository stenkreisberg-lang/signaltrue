What is an active team member?

An active team member is a user whose collaboration data contributes to team-level signals during the billing period. SignalTrue does not bill inactive or disconnected users.

Why teams invest in SignalTrue

Fewer surprise burnout cases

Catch early warning signs before they become resignations.

Reduced meeting overload

Reclaim focus time and improve team velocity.

Faster validation of leadership initiatives

Know if your changes are working in weeks, not quarters.

What SignalTrue does not do

SignalTrue does not track keystrokes, screen activity, message content, or individual behavior. All insights are derived from team-level collaboration patterns and are intended to support leadership decisions, not employee surveillance.
