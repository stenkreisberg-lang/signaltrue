FAQ

What data does SignalTrue use? Primarily Slack and Google Calendar metadata. We focus on patterns, not content, and emphasize privacy-preserving insights.

Who benefits most? Leaders who need early warning on overload and churn risk, and who want to protect deep work time without micromanagement.

Does SignalTrue replace OKRs or project tools? No—SignalTrue complements them by revealing how work actually flows and where it gets stuck.
