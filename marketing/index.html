What SignalTrue actually measures

Five early warning signals, not diagnoses

Recovery time

Hours between last activity and first activity next day. Shrinking recovery windows signal exhaustion risk.

Focus fragmentation

Availability of uninterrupted blocks for deep work. Loss of focus time predicts delays and rework.

Meeting load drift

Sustained increases in meeting hours per person. Creeping overload reduces autonomy and throughput.

Responsiveness pressure

Tightening response-time norms. Increasing reactivity reduces strategic thinking capacity.

Participation imbalance

Concentration of collaboration load. Asymmetry often precedes disengagement and team friction.

These are early warning signals. Not diagnoses.
They indicate when team patterns are drifting from baseline, not individual performance or intent.
