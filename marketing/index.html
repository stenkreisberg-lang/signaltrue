What is a burnout early warning system?

A burnout early warning system tracks leading indicators that change before people quit, disengage, or burn out. SignalTrue measures team-level behavior patterns like meeting overload, recovery time collapse, and after-hours work drift, using privacy-safe metadata from work tools.

Unlike engagement surveys that capture feelings after damage is done, SignalTrue detects baseline drift ‚Äî when a team's normal work patterns shift in a risky direction, gradually, before anyone calls it burnout.
