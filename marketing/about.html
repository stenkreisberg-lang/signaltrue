About SignalTrue

Modern work hides overload until it's too late

By the time burnout appears, the damage is already done. SignalTrue helps leaders see the early behavioral changes that precede breakdown.

The problem we're solving

Distributed work obscures risk

In co-located offices, leaders could see when someone looked exhausted or when meetings were piling up. Remote and hybrid work makes these signals invisible until they become crises.

Surveys are lagging indicators

By the time people report feeling burned out in a survey, the damage is done. Behavioral changes‚Äîshrinking recovery time, eroding focus blocks, rising reactivity‚Äîappear weeks earlier.

Leaders rely on gut feel

Without data, leaders make decisions based on anecdotes, the loudest voices, or their own limited visibility into day-to-day patterns. This creates blind spots and delayed responses.

Overload compounds silently

A few extra meetings per week. Slightly faster response expectations. Incrementally less recovery time. Each change is small, but they compound into systemic overload that breaks execution and wellbeing.
