Frequently asked questions

Is SignalTrue employee monitoring?

No. SignalTrue is a burnout early warning system, not employee monitoring. We analyze team-level behavioral patterns from metadata only. We never read message content, track individuals, or score employee productivity.

Does SignalTrue read Slack messages or email content?

No. SignalTrue uses metadata only ‚Äî timestamps, response patterns, meeting durations. We never access message content, email bodies, or document text. Privacy-first architecture is core to our design.

What are leading indicators of burnout?

Leading indicators of burnout include meeting overload, recovery time collapse, focus fragmentation, after-hours work drift, and responsiveness pressure. These behavioral patterns change before people report feeling burned out in surveys.

How fast can we see early warning signals?

SignalTrue establishes a team baseline within 2-4 weeks of integration. After that, you receive weekly early warning signals showing behavior drift from baseline patterns.

Can managers see individual employee data?

No. SignalTrue shows team-level insights only. We enforce minimum group sizes and automatically redact data that could identify individuals. This protects employee privacy and builds trust.

How is this different from engagement surveys?

Engagement surveys are lagging indicators ‚Äî they measure how people feel after damage is done. SignalTrue detects leading indicators ‚Äî behavioral patterns that change before feelings crystallize into disengagement or burnout.

How is this different from productivity monitoring tools?

Productivity monitoring tracks individual activity and performance. SignalTrue detects team-level burnout risk signals. We don't monitor individuals, score productivity, or track keystrokes. We help leaders prevent burnout, not police employees.

Is this GDPR compliant?

Yes. SignalTrue is designed with GDPR principles: data minimization, purpose limitation, and transparency. We use metadata only, aggregate at team level, and provide clear data controls.
