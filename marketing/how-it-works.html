We observe patterns, not people

SignalTrue detects hidden team strain from work-pattern metadata before it becomes visible as burnout, attrition, manager overload, or execution drag. No content. No individuals. No surveillance.

Connect Google Calendar, Microsoft 365, or Slack. We ingest collaboration and calendar metadata only — never message content, never email bodies, never individual tracking.

  • Metadata only, no content access
  • GDPR-friendly by design

We establish a team baseline

SignalTrue learns your team's sustainable operating patterns over 2-4 weeks. Every team's baseline is unique — no generic industry benchmarks.

  • Team-level aggregation only
  • Minimum group sizes enforced
  • Statistical baseline bands computed per metric

We detect drift

When patterns shift from baseline in risky directions — meetings expand, recovery shrinks, coordination costs rise, focus gets fragmented — we surface it.

  • 7 signal families: Meeting Load, Recovery Erosion, Coordination Strain, Focus Integrity, Team Rhythm, Manager Capacity, Execution Drag
  • Severity levels: Stable → Emerging → Elevated → Critical
  • Persistence-based alerts, not single-spike noise

  • Likely implications spelled out

Seven signal families that detect strain early

These leading indicators change before people report feeling burned out in surveys.

✅ What we detect

  • Meeting Load — meetings expand and crowd out productive work
  • Recovery Erosion — after-hours activity, weekend work, and shrinking recovery windows
  • Coordination Strain — collaboration overhead, reschedule frequency, cross-team dependency
  • Focus Integrity — uninterrupted work blocks, fragmentation, back-to-back density
  • Team Rhythm Stability — week-to-week volatility and pattern instability
  • Manager Capacity Risk — manager overload, calendar fragmentation, and routing bottlenecks
  • Execution Drag — hidden operating friction from combined signal pressure

🚫 What we never do

  • We do not read message content
  • We do not read email bodies
  • We do not expose individuals in insight surfaces
  • We do not monitor keystrokes, screens, or webcams
  • We do not create covert employee surveillance scores
  • We do not rank or score individual employees
  • We do not monitor productivity
