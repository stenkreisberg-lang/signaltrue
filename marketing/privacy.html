SignalTrue uses Google Workspace APIs to provide behavioral and collaboration insights related to meeting and calendar usage. When a user connects their Google Workspace account, SignalTrue accesses the following Google user data:

Data accessed

  • Google Calendar metadata, including meeting invitations, response status (accepted, declined, tentative), timestamps, and availability signals
  • Basic account information required for authentication and identification within SignalTrue
  • Organizational account context (non-consumer Google Workspace accounts only)

SignalTrue does not access the content of emails, email bodies, attachments, files, or calendar event descriptions unless explicitly required for core functionality. Personal Google accounts (@gmail.com) are not supported.

Data usage

The accessed Google Calendar data is used solely to:

  • Analyze response behavior to meeting invitations, such as acceptance speed and response patterns
  • Identify behavioral trends and changes over time related to collaboration and scheduling
  • Provide aggregated insights and visualizations within the SignalTrue platform to authenticated users and their organizations

SignalTrue does not use Google user data for advertising purposes.

Data storage and security

Google user data is processed and stored securely using industry-standard security measures. Data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access is restricted to authorized systems required to operate the SignalTrue service. Data is retained only for as long as the user maintains an active account or connected integration, or as required to deliver the service.

Data sharing

SignalTrue does not sell, rent, or share Google user data with third parties. Google user data is not shared outside of SignalTrue except with trusted infrastructure providers strictly necessary to operate the service, under contractual confidentiality and security obligations.

AI and model training

Google user data accessed via Google Workspace APIs is not used to train generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models. Any analysis performed is limited to user-specific or organization-specific insights required to deliver the SignalTrue service.
