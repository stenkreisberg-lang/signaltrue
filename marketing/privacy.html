SignalTrue complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. When you connect a Google Workspace account, we adhere to the following practices:

1. Data Accessed

SignalTrue accesses the following specific Google user data types:

  • Google Calendar: Event metadata (title, description, start/end time, location), attendees, organizer, response status (accepted/declined/tentative), and recurring event information.
  • Google Profile: Email address and basic profile information for authentication and domain verification.

We do not access Google Drive files, Gmail message content, or other Google Workspace data types not listed above.

2. How we use Google user data

We use the accessed Google data solely to provide the SignalTrue service features:

  • Team Health Analytics: We use Calendar event metadata to compute meeting load, focus time, and response latency trends at the team level.
  • Collaboration Insights: We analyze response patterns to identify potential burnout risks and collaboration overload.

What we do NOT do:

  • We do not use Google data to monitor individual employee performance for surveillance purposes.
  • We do not use Google data for advertising purposes.
  • We do not use Google user data to train generalized artificial intelligence or machine learning models.

3. Data Sharing

SignalTrue does not sell Google user data. We do not share Google user data with third parties, except for the following service providers strictly required to operate the application:

  • Hosting & Infrastructure: Render, Vercel (for application hosting and delivery).
  • Database: MongoDB Atlas (for secure data storage).

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and only process it in accordance with our instructions.

4. Storage and Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL) and at rest (AES-256).
  • Access Control: Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel on a least-privilege basis.

5. Retention and Deletion

Retention: We retain Google user data only for as long as you maintain an active account or connected integration.

Deletion:

  • Automatic: When you disconnect the Google integration or delete your SignalTrue account, all associated synchronized data is scheduled for deletion.
  • Request: You may request immediate deletion of your data by contacting us at support@signaltrue.ai. We will process deletion requests within 30 days.
