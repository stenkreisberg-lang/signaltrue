# CEO / Executive FAQ

## Strategic Value

### What is SignalTrue in one sentence?

SignalTrue provides team behavioral intelligence that helps you understand organizational health and collaboration patterns without surveillance or privacy concerns.

### Why should I care about this?

As a CEO, you face challenges that SignalTrue addresses:

1. **Blind spots in organizational health** - By the time you hear about team problems, they're often critical
2. **Remote/hybrid visibility** - Traditional management signals don't work in distributed teams
3. **Burnout and retention** - Early warning signs are invisible without the right data
4. **Data-driven decisions** - Workforce decisions shouldn't rely on gut feel alone

### What's different about SignalTrue?

Unlike monitoring tools that create surveillance and erode trust, SignalTrue:
- Analyzes only metadata, never message content
- Works at team level only (min 5 people)
- Provides organizational insights without individual tracking
- Maintains employee trust through transparent methodology

### Is this employee monitoring?

**No.** This is explicitly not monitoring. We do not:
- Read messages or emails
- Track individual productivity
- Create individual performance scores
- Enable surveillance of any kind

## Trust and Culture

### How do employees feel about this?

When communicated transparently, employees typically respond positively because:
- It's clearly not surveillance
- It focuses on team health, not individual tracking
- It shows leadership cares about sustainable work
- The privacy protections are genuine

### Won't this damage trust?

The opposite. SignalTrue helps demonstrate:
- Commitment to privacy (no content access)
- Focus on team wellbeing (not individual tracking)
- Data-driven care (not surveillance)

The key is transparent communication about what SignalTrue is and isn't.

### How do we communicate this to the organization?

We recommend:
1. Be transparent about what SignalTrue does
2. Emphasize the privacy protections
3. Focus on the team health benefits
4. Explain that individuals are never tracked
5. Invite questions and feedback

## Risk and Compliance

### What's the compliance situation?

SignalTrue is designed for compliance:
- GDPR compliant by design
- No individual data processing
- Privacy impact assessment available
- Works council friendly (not surveillance)

### What's the risk if this goes wrong?

Risks are minimized by design:
- No content access means no content exposure
- Team-level aggregation means no individual data
- Read-only access limits blast radius
- Instant disconnection capability

### Can competitors or regulators access this data?

No. All data is:
- Encrypted and protected
- Only accessible to authorized users
- Never shared with third parties
- Deletable upon request
