# What SignalTrue Does NOT Do

## Purpose of This Document

This document explicitly states what SignalTrue does not do. This is critical for:
- Setting clear expectations
- Building trust with employees and stakeholders
- Ensuring accurate communication
- Differentiating from monitoring tools

## Content Access: NEVER

### We do NOT read messages

- No Slack message content
- No email bodies or subjects
- No Teams chat content
- No Google Chat message text

### We do NOT access documents

- No file contents
- No Google Docs/Sheets
- No SharePoint/OneDrive files
- No attachments

### We do NOT see communications

- No meeting transcripts
- No call recordings
- No video content
- No presentation content

## Individual Tracking: NEVER

### We do NOT track individual behavior

- No per-person activity metrics
- No individual productivity scores
- No personal performance ratings
- No individual time tracking

### We do NOT enable surveillance

- No keystroke logging
- No screen monitoring
- No application usage tracking
- No website browsing history
- No location tracking

### We do NOT create individual profiles

- No behavioral profiles per employee
- No individual pattern analysis
- No personal performance trending
- No individual comparison or ranking

## Data Usage Restrictions: STRICT

### We do NOT use data for training

- No machine learning model training on customer data
- No AI improvement using customer data
- No algorithm development from customer information

### We do NOT share data

- No selling of data
- No third-party data sharing (except essential sub-processors)
- No cross-customer data analysis
- No benchmarking without explicit consent

### We do NOT retain unnecessarily

- Configurable retention periods
- Full deletion upon request
- No hidden data copies
- No backup retention beyond policy

## Minimum Aggregation: ALWAYS

### We ALWAYS aggregate to team level

- Minimum 5 people per analysis group
- No way to identify individuals
- No "drill down" to person level
- Statistical anonymity guaranteed

### We NEVER expose individual data

- Even if asked
- Even if technically possible
- Even to administrators
- This is architecturally enforced

## What This Means in Practice

### For Employees

You can be confident that:
- Your messages are never read
- Your individual work isn't tracked
- You're not being surveilled
- You're part of a team aggregate, never singled out

### For Managers

You will:
- See team-level patterns only
- Never access individual metrics
- Get insights without surveillance
- Build trust through transparency

### For IT/Security

You can verify that:
- Only metadata permissions requested
- No content access capabilities
- Read-only integration
- Instant revocation possible

### For HR

You can communicate that:
- This is not monitoring
- Individual privacy is protected
- No performance surveillance
- Focus is on team wellbeing

## Our Commitment

SignalTrue commits to never:

1. Access message or document content
2. Track individual employees
3. Enable surveillance capabilities
4. Use data for model training
5. Share data with unauthorized parties
6. Expose individual-level data
7. Violate the team minimum (5 people)
8. Misrepresent our capabilities

If we ever discover a bug or behavior that violates these principles, we commit to:
- Immediately notifying affected customers
- Fixing the issue with highest priority
- Conducting a root cause analysis
- Implementing preventive measures
